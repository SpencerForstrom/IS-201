My Story

I was born in Provo, Utah on August 14th, 2001. My incredible parents, Timothy and Michelle, raised me in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. They taught me the value of hardwork, patience, and faith. When I turned 18, I served as a fulltime missionary in both the Dominican Republic, Santo Domingo West and Los Angeles missions. Upon completeion, I began my journey at Brigham Young University. Now as a sophomore studying Information Systems, I look back at all the blessings I've experienced. I'm very grateful for this wonderful life I've been given and hope to live it to the fullest.

My life goals are to remain a faithful disciple of Christ, to find and marry my wife, treating her like a queen at all times, to raise my children in the Gospel, to provide for my family, and to never pass up an opportunity to help someone in need. Thanks for taking the time to learn a little more about me! If you're interested to get to know me further, please use the contact information below or send me a message directly.
